Paternoster

Paternoster is a quaint little fishing village located on the West Coast of South Africa.

Voorstrand Beach

Voorstrand is the main beach in Paternoster and is very popular during peak seasons.

Beach House

The Beach House is self-catering accommodation and has 3 bedrooms, each with on-suite bathrooms. With a fireplace, pool and a great view 20m from Voorstrand Beach, it has everything you need.

Langstrand Beach

Langstrand is a quiet beach on the northern side of the bay in Mosselbank, Paternoster.

Shalom Beach House

Located 50m from Langstrand Beach, Shalom is a lovely cottage with a great view. This self-catering cottage has a fire-place, 3 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, and is pet-friendly.
