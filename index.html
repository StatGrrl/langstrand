Paternoster

Paternoster is a quaint little fishing village located on the West Coast of South Africa. A number of activities are available such as surfing, kite surfing, wind-surfing, horse-riding, as well as cheese, beer and wine tasting.

Voorstrand Beach

Voorstrand is the main beach in Paternoster and is very popular during peak seasons. There is also a restaurant on the beach front.

Beach House

The Beach House is self-catering accommodation and has 3 bedrooms, each with on-suite bathrooms. With a fireplace, braai, pool and a great view 20m from Voorstrand Beach, it has everything you need.

Langstrand Beach

Langstrand is a quiet beach on the northern side of the bay in Mosselbank, Paternoster. It is also a popular beach for dog walking.

Shalom House

Located 50m from Langstrand Beach, Shalom is a lovely cottage with a great view. This self-catering cottage has a fire-place, braai, 3 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, and is pet-friendly.

Location

Paternoster is located on the West Coast near to St Helena Bay, Velddrif, Jacobs Bay, Saldanha, and Langebaan. It is 162 km from Cape Town International Airport and approximately 2 hours by car, making it an ideal holiday destination.
